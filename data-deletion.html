This page explains how to request deletion of your data from the ImgCompress WhatsApp bot ("the bot", "we", "us").

What we store

The images you send are processed in memory or temporary storage and deleted right after the compressed result is sent back. We do not keep a copy of your images. We may hold short lived technical logs (file size, image format, timestamps, your WhatsApp identifier) used only to run and debug the service. These logs are deleted automatically after a short period. See our Privacy Policy for full detail.

How to request deletion

Because images are deleted right after compression, there is normally nothing stored to delete. If you still want us to delete any remaining data tied to your WhatsApp account, follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to imgcompress@dayamoy.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include the WhatsApp phone number you used with the bot so we can locate any data tied to it.
  3. We will delete any data we hold for that number and email you a confirmation within 30 days.

Stop using the bot

You can stop at any time by simply not sending the bot any more images. You can also block the bot in WhatsApp.
